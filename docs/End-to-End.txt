Assuming the system is fully deployed, it runs like this:
Operator presses Start Engine in the dashboard.
System loads runtime config, DB settings, trading mode, risk limits, approved symbols, and service health.
Data feeds connect: MT5/broker feed, currency strength, COT/macro data, market candles, account state.
Scanner checks approved symbols such as XAUUSD, EURUSD, GBPJPY, indices, etc.
Intelligence layer scores market structure, macro bias, COT positioning, currency strength, regime, volatility, and historical similarity.
AI Decision Engine creates a hybrid decision: Execute, NoTrade, or Manual Review.
The 85% confidence gate blocks weak setups.
Risk gate checks drawdown, daily loss, spread, slippage, news risk, risk/reward, and strategy health.
If approved, execution routes the order through the MT5 bridge.
Broker fill is confirmed.
Trade monitoring starts: floating P/L, stop loss, profit lock, risk controls, exit rules.
Exit engine decides whether to hold, partially close, trail, or close.
Closed trade outcome is persisted.
Reports, audit trail, decision history, and learning feedback are updated.
